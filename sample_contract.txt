2. Priya Sharma, residing at Bangalore, Karnataka, India
   PAN: FGHIJ5678K, Aadhaar: XXXX-XXXX-9012

(collectively referred to as "Founders")

WHEREAS the Founders wish to establish TechInnovations Private Limited ("Company") under
the Companies Act, 2013, with its registered office in Bangalore, Karnataka, India.

1. EQUITY DISTRIBUTION

1.1 The authorized share capital shall be INR 10,00,000 divided into 10,00,000 equity
shares of INR 1 each.

1.2 Initial equity distribution:
    - Rajesh Kumar: 60% (600,000 shares)
    - Priya Sharma: 40% (400,000 shares)

1.3 All shares shall be issued as per the provisions of the Companies Act, 2013.

2. VESTING SCHEDULE

2.1 All equity shall vest over a period of 4 (four) years from the date of incorporation.

2.2 25% of the shares shall vest after completion of 1 year ("Cliff Period") and the
remaining 75% shall vest monthly over the subsequent 36 months.

2.3 Upon termination of a Founder's involvement with the Company before completion of
vesting, unvested shares shall be subject to buyback at par value as per Section 68
of the Companies Act, 2013.

3. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY ASSIGNMENT

3.1 All Founders hereby assign to the Company all rights, title and interest in any and
all intellectual property created in connection with the Company's business.

3.2 This assignment is made pursuant to Section 16 and 17 of the Indian Copyright Act,
1957, and includes all patents, trademarks, copyrights, trade secrets, and other
proprietary rights.

3.3 Each Founder warrants that the IP assigned does not infringe any third-party rights.

4. ROLES AND RESPONSIBILITIES

4.1 Rajesh Kumar shall serve as Chief Executive Officer (CEO)
4.2 Priya Sharma shall serve as Chief Technology Officer (CTO)
4.3 Major decisions shall require unanimous consent of all Founders

5. NON-COMPETE AND NON-SOLICITATION

5.1 The Founders acknowledge that under Section 27 of the Indian Contract Act, 1872,
restraints on trade during the term of partnership are permissible but post-termination
non-compete clauses are generally unenforceable.

5.2 During the term of this Agreement, Founders shall not engage in any competing business.

5.3 Founders agree not to solicit Company employees or customers for 12 months post-exit.

6. CONFIDENTIALITY

6.1 All Founders shall maintain strict confidentiality of proprietary information for
a period of 3 years following termination.

7. DISPUTE RESOLUTION

7.1 Any disputes shall first be attempted to be resolved through good faith negotiations.

7.2 If negotiations fail, disputes shall be resolved through arbitration under the
Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996.

7.3 Arbitration shall be conducted in Bangalore, India with 3 arbitrators.

8. GOVERNING LAW

8.1 This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of India.

8.2 The courts of Bangalore, Karnataka shall have exclusive jurisdiction over any disputes.

9. STAMP DUTY

9.1 This Agreement shall be duly stamped as per the Indian Stamp Act and the stamp laws
applicable in Karnataka.

9.2 Stamp duty charges shall be borne equally by all Founders.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Agreement on the date first written above.
